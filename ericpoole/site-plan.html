Purpose

Have you ever wanted to go white water rafting? Want to go to a place where you can rent a GoPro, and send the footage straight to Youtube? We offer all the supplies you need! Thanks to our close relationship with RedBul we offer the lowest price on their products. We have white water rafting equipment, camping spots, and free firewood. Invite friends, and we will offer a 10% discount for each person that comes along with you. You can use our services to send you pictures of you and your friends going down the river. We can provide training of the fundamentals to white water rafting, or we can send you along on your own.

About Me

About the Author Eric Poole grew up in Hillsboro Oregon, where he spent a large amount of his time volunteering, and spending time falling in love with the great outdoors. He would love to hike bikes and do anything as long as it was outside. He had his first 50 mile backpacking experience. He also got his Ham Radio License there. He was also a large part of the scouting community there. He would network with the people in his area, and work to help younger scouts to get there merit badges. Eric Moved to a large number of places after that including California, Salt Lake City Utah, and eventually Idaho. Where he has picked up the bass.
